ERBORIAN REBRANDING

creative direction and branding

Korean/Parisian beauty brand Erborian asked me to take over Creative Direction and lead their rebranding for the US and Canadian markets. So I worked closely with the brand’s team to redesign all assets. The direction for the brand was then shifted into a modern, youthful direction, engaging a younger/middle-aged customer with playful colors, bold imagery, while showcasing the product’s rich textures.

imagery

I wanted to elevate the imagery with the use of high contrast shadows. Showing the products in a lifestyle way, I also wanted the images to have movement and depth.
